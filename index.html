Data Scientist

A data scientist acts as the "analytical brain" behind data-driven decision-making, using data to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable insights. Their work often informs business strategy and creates the models that AI engineers later operationalize. Key Responsibilities: Gathering and cleaning data for analysis. Analyzing data to find patterns and relationships. Building predictive models using statistical and machine learning techniques. Presenting data insights and recommendations to others.
